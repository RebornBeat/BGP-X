# BGP-X Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

BGP-X is a project built around the principle that people deserve privacy. That mission extends to how we treat each other.

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all contributors, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all communication
- Critique ideas, not people
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Prioritize the health of the project and the safety of the people it serves
- Be honest about what you do and do not know

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or threats of any kind
- Demeaning, discriminatory, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Using BGP-X infrastructure or community for surveillance or harm
- Trolling or sustained disruption of discussions

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct should be reported to the project maintainers.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.

Enforcement actions may include:
- Private warning
- Temporary ban from contribution or communication channels
- Permanent ban
